# 01. Consumables

In a Mota game, consumables are the potions and gems placed on the map. Default examples live under `/Data/Blueprints/Consumables/`.

## Potions

Potion blueprints derive from `Source.Consumables.Bottle.Bottle`. Picking one up restores the player's HP. The built-in `Battler` HP monitor automatically clamps the restored value to `[0, MAXHP]`.

Set `HP_plus` to control how much HP is restored. For example, `Bottle_200.json` and `Bottle_500.json` use different values. To add a new potion, duplicate an existing bottle blueprint and change this field; adjust `texturePath` and `defaultRect` when you need a different appearance.

## Gems

Gem blueprints derive from `Source.Consumables.Gem.Gem`. Picking one up increases a chosen combat attribute.

- `ATTR_key`: the attribute to raise, such as `ATK` or `DEF`.
- `plus`: the amount to add.

For example, `Gem_1_1.json` raises `ATK` by `1`. When authoring a new gem, change these two fields on top of an example; visuals are controlled through `texturePath` and `defaultRect` as usual.
