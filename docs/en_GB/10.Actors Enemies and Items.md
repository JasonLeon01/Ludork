# 10. Actors, Enemies, and Items

This chapter covers RPG objects that the Ludork default template already provides.

## Player

Braver is the default player. Core values include HP, max HP, attack, defence, EXP, gold, level, and job. The player can hold items, equipment, states, and recorded floors.

## Enemies

The Ludork default template includes slime and skeleton soldier. Enemy data contains name, description, HP, attack, defence, EXP, gold, drops, and specials. After placing enemies on a map, test contact, battle result, and rewards.

## Doors and Keys

The Ludork default template has yellow, blue, and red keys with matching doors, plus a general door. When testing doors, check key count, door sound, opening animation, passability, and state after loading a save.

## Items

Current items include enemy book, floor teleport, Pickaxe, Earthquake Scroll, Snowflake, and Ice Axe. Consumable items reduce count when used; utility items open windows or change terrain.

## Consumables and Equipment

Red/blue gems and recovery bottles change player values. The basic sword and shield test equipment slots. After changing values, check the menu, HUD, enemy book, and battle result.
