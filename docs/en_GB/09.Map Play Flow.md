# 09. Map Play Flow

Ludork default template gameplay happens mainly in `Map_01.dat` and `Map_02.dat`. A map contains terrain, actors, lights, audio, weather, fog, and interactive objects.

## Start Flow

After starting a new game, the player appears on `Map_01.dat` at `(6, 12)`. Check movement, camera follow, and the HUD first, then test doors, items, enemies, and teleport points on the map.

## Map Changes

Upstairs and downstairs teleport points change maps and record floor teleport points. After visiting a teleport point, open the floor teleport window to view recorded floors and jump between them.

When changing stairs or map links, test both directions and confirm target map, landing point, and transition behavior.

## Terrain Changes

Pickaxe, Earthquake Scroll, Snowflake, and Ice Axe alter map terrain. The changes are saved. When testing them, save, load, and re-enter the map to verify persistence.

## Interactions

Doors usually need keys or conditions. Enemies show values and participate in battle checks. Dialogue actors open messages or selections. Shops, enemy book, and floor teleport temporarily pause player movement and restore it after closing.

## Lighting

Ambient light, map lights, and blueprint lights stack together. After editing lighting, check dark areas, bright areas, moving actors, and open windows so important information remains readable.
