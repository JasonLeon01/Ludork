# 13. Resources, Audio, and Effects

This chapter describes common Ludork default template resources and visual effects.

## Images

System images are in `Assets/System`, actor images in `Assets/Characters`, and icons in `Assets/Icons`. After replacing an image, select it again in the related blueprint, general data, or system setting.

Actors and doors often rely on fixed tile sizes. After replacing images, check default crop, animation frames, and map collision alignment.

## Fonts

The default font is `HarmonyOS_Sans_SC_Regular.ttf`. If you replace it, test Chinese, English, numbers, menus, dialogue, and save details.

## Sound and Music

Common sounds are in `Assets/Sounds`, and title music is in `Assets/Musics`. Audio settings cover cursor, confirm, cancel, error, shop, save, load, door, stairs, item, and equipment sounds.

Maps can also use background music or ambience. Test title screen, map changes, menu open/close, and volume settings separately.

## Visual Effects

The Ludork default template includes transitions, flash, shake, weather, fog, material settings, and map lighting. Effect assets mainly live in `Assets/Transitions`, `Assets/Fogs`, and `Assets/Shaders`.

After changing effects, test regular maps, dark maps, open windows, automatic pathing, and map changes so the screen does not hide important information.

## Animations

Attack animation uses `Data/Animations` and `Assets/Animations`. After replacing animation images or sounds, test placement, duration, sound timing, and scale.
